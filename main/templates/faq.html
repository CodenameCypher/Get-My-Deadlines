Frequently Asked Questions

Why should you use Get My Deadlines ?

Get My Deadlines will surf through your buX courses and scrape your assessment deadlines from your courses, and show it all here in a single page, so you don't have to do it manually.

Is it safe?

Yes, it is. We are not keeping your buX credentials. Don't believe us? Check our source code - GitHub.
Don't worry. We don't have enough time to get in your buX to see your missed deadlines and 10% progress.

  • Django Framework
  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript
  • WebScrapping (beautifulsoup)
  • WebCrawling
